# Customer Success — Mighty Pro Salaries at Mighty Networks

Research compiled April 2026 from public job postings, Glassdoor, Levels.fyi,
Salary.com, Indeed, and Comparably.

## TL;DR

| Role (Mighty Pro-facing) | Base salary range (USD) |
| --- | --- |
| Customer Success Advocate | ~$47K – $66K (avg ~$59K) |
| Customer Success Manager, Mighty Pro | $70,000 – $125,000 |
| Customer Success Strategist (Mighty Pro) | $80,000 – $130,000 |
| Technical Customer Success Manager | $91,000 – $139,000 |

All ranges are base salary only. Final offers vary with location, experience,
and cost of living. Roles are remote-first in the US.

## Role-by-role detail

### Customer Success Manager, Mighty Pro
- **Base salary:** $70,000 – $125,000 (source: Mighty Networks / Greycroft /
  EdTech.com job posting).
- **Scope:** Primary partner to Mighty Pro Hosts (450+ brands, creators, and
  entrepreneurs on the Mighty Pro plan). Responsible for proactive engagement,
  strategic guidance, and relationship management to drive community outcomes.
- **Benefits:** Remote-first culture; health, vision, dental, PTO; stated
  leadership pathway and skill-development support.

### Customer Success Strategist (Mighty Pro)
- **Base salary:** $80,000 – $130,000 (source: Mighty Networks careers page /
  Built In listing).
- **Scope:** Works with premium Mighty Pro customers to design engaging
  community experiences tied to business outcomes. More strategic/consultative
  lean than the CSM role.

### Customer Success Advocate
- **Average salary:** ~$59,081/yr; typical band $53,176 – $65,034 (Salary.com).
- **Glassdoor range:** ~$47K – $66K/yr.
- **Hourly equivalent:** ~$28/hr base (as of March 2026).
- **Scope:** Front-line support for Mighty Networks customers, including
  Mighty Pro accounts. Lower-tenure entry point into the Customer Success org.

### Technical Customer Success Manager
- **Salary range:** $91,000 – $139,000/yr (Glassdoor).
- **Scope:** Technical overlay on the CSM function — integrations, API,
  platform configuration for higher-complexity Mighty Pro hosts.

## Company-wide salary context

- Mighty Networks average total comp: ~$124K (base ~$116K + ~$8K bonus)
  across 63 reported salaries on Glassdoor.
- Company-wide range: $68.6K (Customer Service, US) to $199,996 (Software
  Engineer, Canada) per Levels.fyi.
- Customer Support department average total comp: ~$71K.
- Community Manager average: ~$67,678/yr (Glassdoor).

## What "Mighty Pro" means for compensation

Mighty Pro is the company's flagship, highest-tier plan (premium strategy,
software, AI, and white-glove service for 450+ creator/entrepreneur brands).
Mighty Pro-facing Customer Success roles sit at the top of the CS salary
band because they manage the highest-ARR accounts and carry strategic
responsibility. The pay ladder inside Customer Success looks roughly like:

```
Customer Success Advocate  ($47–66K)
        ↓
Customer Success Manager, Mighty Pro  ($70–125K)
        ↓
Customer Success Strategist, Mighty Pro  ($80–130K)
        ↓
Technical Customer Success Manager  ($91–139K)
```

Benefits package (remote-first, health/vision/dental, PTO, growth pathways)
is consistent across the CS org.

## Caveats

- Mighty Networks is privately held; equity grants are not disclosed in the
  public postings reviewed.
- Bonus/variable comp is referenced at the company-average level (~$8K) but
  is not broken out per CS role in the public data.
- Ranges reflect US postings. International pay may differ.
